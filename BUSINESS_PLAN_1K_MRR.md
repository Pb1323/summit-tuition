# Path to £1,000 MRR — Realistic Plan

Last updated: 2026-07-10. Builds on `COMPETITOR_GAP_ANALYSIS.md` and `MARKETING_CHANNELS.md`.

## Reality check on the numbers
- Pure passive (subscription only) is the **slowest** path with zero traffic/reviews today — competitors like EdifyPod already sit at <£20/mo with more features, so a cold subscriber funnel takes months to build.
- Active tutoring converts **fastest** because you can close a deal in one phone call, at £35-50/hr per the UK market rate research. 3-4 regular students (2hrs/week each) = ~£800-1,500/mo alone.
- Realistic fastest route to £1k MRR is **blended**, not either/or: a handful of active tutoring clients now, subscription passive income compounding underneath.

## The funnel (uses what already exists)
1. **Free or £15-19 diagnostic assessment** as the lead magnet — low friction, already built (`diagnostic` mock), and cheap enough that community outreach (temples, Chinese schools) doesn't feel like a sales pitch.
2. Every diagnostic ends with a recommendation: **Mock Club subscription** (passive, self-serve) or **1:1 tutoring** (active, higher £ per family, capped by your hours).
3. **Ask every satisfied family for a review** immediately — research confirms reviews are the #1 trust signal parents check before booking. You have zero reviews today; this is the biggest lever available and costs nothing.

## 90-day milestones
| Weeks | Action | Target |
|---|---|---|
| 1-2 | Run 3-5 free/discounted diagnostics via personal network + one Chinese school or temple contact (from `MARKETING_CHANNELS.md`). Set up Google Business Profile. | First 3-5 reviews |
| 3-4 | Convert pilot families to paying (tutoring or subscription). Post 1-2x/week of *value* (not ads) in permitted WhatsApp/Facebook parent groups — tips, not pitches. | 2 tutoring clients + 5 subscribers |
| 5-8 | Expand outreach to 2-3 more community contacts. Add a referral perk (e.g. free week for referring a friend) — zero-cost passive growth. | 3-4 tutoring clients + 12-15 subscribers |
| 9-12 | Local SEO landing pages live (from earlier SEO work) start contributing organic leads. Push VR/NVR content live — it's the market's proven gap. | 4 tutoring clients (~£800-1,200/mo) + 20 subscribers (~£400-500/mo) ≈ **£1,000+ MRR** |

## What NOT to do
- Don't compete with EdifyPod on price — you'll lose. Compete on trust (manual marking, transparent single-company billing, no access-cutoff risk) — a documented gap competitors are failing on.
- Don't spam community WhatsApp/Facebook groups — most explicitly ban tutor self-promotion and it burns the relationship fast. One value-post per week, max.
- Don't build more subscription tiers before you have ~10 paying customers proving the funnel works — polish the one path first.

## Single highest-leverage next step
Get the **first 3 reviews** — everything else (community trust, conversion rate, referrals) compounds from having social proof that currently doesn't exist at all.
