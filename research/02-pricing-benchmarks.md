# UK 11+ Market Pricing Benchmarks

*Market research for Summit Tuition (Oxford), compiled July 2026. Every price is as displayed or reported at research date — spot-check before using in pricing decks. Prices inc VAT unless noted.*

---

## 1. One-to-one 11+ tutoring (per hour)

The national picture is a broad £40–80/hr band for experienced private 11+ tutors, with London, the South East and grammar-heavy counties (Bucks, Kent) at the top ([TutorLab — Private Tutor Rates UK 2026](https://www.tutorlab.uk/research/private-tutor-rates-uk)). For London/South East specifically, typical experienced 11+ rates are **£60–80/hr**, with specialist tutors in super-selective areas charging **£80–120/hr**; a weekly programme lands at **£3,000–4,500+ per year** ([TutorLab](https://www.tutorlab.uk/research/private-tutor-rates-uk), corroborated by [Atom Learning's 11+ tutoring cost guide](https://www.atomlearning.com/blog/11-plus-tutoring-cost), which models ~50 sessions at a ~£55/hr UK average ≈ £2,750/year).

Marketplace rates sit well below agency rates — the Oxford spread is striking:

- **Superprof (Oxford):** 11+ tutors from £15/hr, average ~£27/hr ([Superprof Oxford 11+](https://www.superprof.co.uk/lessons/11-school-exam-preparation/oxford/))
- **Tutorfair (Oxford):** average £32/hr for 11+ ([Tutorfair Oxford](https://www.tutorfair.com/11-plus-tutor-oxford))
- **MyTutor (national, online):** tutors set rates £22–55/hr; most 11+ lessons £26–40/hr; pay-as-you-go, no subscription ([MyTutor pricing](https://www.mytutor.co.uk/pricing), [MyTutor 11+ tutors](https://www.mytutor.co.uk/view-tutors/11-plus))
- **Premium agencies serving Oxford schools:** Owl Tutors — which lists 9 tutors with Magdalen College School prep experience — starts around **£70/hr and up** ([Tutorfair Oxford page noting premium rates "from £70.00"](https://www.tutorfair.com/11-plus-tutor-oxford), [Owl Tutors MCS guide](https://owltutors.co.uk/schools/magdalen-college-school/))

**Benchmark takeaway:** Oxford's *visible* market clears at £27–32/hr (marketplace) but the same families pay £70+/hr to London agencies for independent-school prep. A local premium specialist can credibly price 1:1 at **£50–75/hr** — above marketplace, below/at agency — if it carries institutional trust (reports, mock data, school-specific track record).

## 2. Group tuition (per session)

- **The Exam Coach:** live online group 11+ workshops **£20/session**; 5-day intensive holiday courses **£299** (listed against a £650 "original" price) ([The Exam Coach store](https://www.theexamcoach.tv/courses))
- **Explore Learning:** 11+/entrance-exam membership **from ~£21/session**; packaged as ~**£175/month weekly** or **£255/month twice-weekly** for 11+ streams (~1h15 sessions, varies by centre) ([Explore Learning pricing](https://www.explorelearning.co.uk/pricing/), figures via [MyEngineeringBuddy review roundup](https://www.myengineeringbuddy.com/blog/explore-learning-reviews-alternatives-pricing-offerings/))
- **Kip McGrath:** ~**£35–36 per 60-min session** average, once weekly; Oxford delivered via their online arm ([Kip McGrath pricing](https://www.kipmcgrath.co.uk/pricing), [Kip McGrath Oxford](https://www.kipmcgrath.co.uk/oxford))
- **Kumon (adjacent anchor):** ~**£70/month per subject** + ~£35 registration ([Kumon UK media room](https://www.kumon.co.uk/media-room), [Kumon Lowton centre article, May 2026](https://lowtonandgolbornenews.co.uk/start-your-child-early-with-kumon-and-help-them-reach-their-full-potential/))
- **Adnan Khan Tutoring:** 4-week 11+ summer booster (12 group sessions) **£599** ≈ £50/session, two-instalment payment offered ([Adnan Khan summer course](https://www.adnankhantutoring.co.uk/11-plus-summer-booster-course/))
- **11plustutoring.uk (online group memberships):** **£149/month for two weekly sessions, £199/month for three** ([11plustutoring.uk Oxford page](https://www.11plustutoring.uk/11-plus-exams/town/11-plus-tutoring-oxford/))
- **Oxford Tutors UK (budget online group):** £11–18/hr depending on group size 2–4 ([oxfordtutorsuk.com prices](https://oxfordtutorsuk.com/prices/))

**Benchmark takeaway:** small-group 11+ clusters at **£20–36/session**, i.e. £80–150/month for weekly attendance. Intensive holiday courses command **£300–600 per week-long block** — the highest-margin format in the market.

## 3. Online platforms & mock subscriptions (monthly/annual)

- **Atom Learning** — the price ceiling: **£39.99/mo** (core subjects), **£59.99/mo** (Exam Prep), **£69.99/mo** (Exam Prep Plus, incl. mock tests + exam planning); **annual saves ~20%** (≈ £32–56/mo effective) ([Atom pricing](https://www.atomlearning.com/pricing))
- **BOFA 11+** — the price floor: **£9.50/mo rolling**, no tie-in; fixed 3/6/9/12-month one-off payments; school-partner deals of 7 months/£40 and 15 months/£70 ([BOFA subscriptions](https://www.bofa11plus.com/bofahelp/subscriptions-explained), [BOFA school offer](https://www.bofa11plus.com/content/offer-subscription)); separate mock-test packs **sold in 3s** ([BOFA help](https://www.bofa11plus.com/bofahelp/difference-11plus-pretest))
- **CGP 11+ Online:** monthly auto-renewing subscription, **yearly plan saves 30%**; exact £ not shown in listings (book-brand pricing, i.e. low) ([CGP 11+ Online](https://www.cgpbooks.co.uk/11-plus-13-plus-books/11/11-plus-online-subscriptions))
- **Online one-off mock tests:** Chuckra **Virtual Mock £35** (two tests, all four subjects, at home) and single-subject **Readiness Mock £20** ([Chuckra pricing](https://www.11plus.co.uk/pricing/)); GLECTA advertises online 11+ mocks **from £20/subject** ([GLECTA](https://glecta.com/11plus-mock-exams-details)); PiAcademy sells GL mock packs (one attempt, instant results, 1-year validity — prices in checkout only) ([PiAcademy mocks](https://piacademy.co.uk/mock-exams/))
- **School-specific online test packs (independent-school skew):** Pretest Plus sells per-school test bundles discounted 25–33%, with advertised savings of **£61–£226 per bundle** — implying flagship bundles in the £150–450+ range; 10% off orders over £80 ([Pretest Plus bundles](https://pretestplus.co.uk/product/london-11-plus-consortium-practice-tests-bundle/), [Cheltenham Ladies' bundle "save £226"](https://pretestplus.co.uk/product/cheltenham-ladies-college-11-plus-exam-practice-tests-1-10-bundle/))

**Benchmark takeaway:** the subscription market is barbell-shaped — £9.50/mo (BOFA) vs £40–70/mo (Atom) with little in between. A **£25–45/mo** platform with genuinely better mock reporting slots into an empty middle; premium mock events can be priced per-sitting on top.

## 4. In-person mock exam day fees

- **Marie Redmond Tuition (Bucks/Slough/Reading/SW Herts):** **£80 for one mock**, sliding to £70/£65/£60/£55 each at 2/3/4/5 exams and **£50 each beyond five** (e.g. 5 for £275); 2h30 exam-hall sittings, results in 48h with per-question video explanations and cohort ranking ([Marie Redmond Bucks mocks](https://marieredmond.co.uk/mock-exams/buckinghamshire-11-plus-mock-exams-gl-assessment-style/))
- **Chuckra test-centre mock day: £70** inc VAT ([Chuckra pricing](https://www.11plus.co.uk/pricing/))
- **Susan Daughtrey Education:** exam-hall mocks (Bucks GL-style, Latymer, SW Herts); base fee in booking flow, **+£10 supplement** for versions including a creative-writing assignment; £20 cancellation fee ([SDE Latymer mocks](https://susandaughtreyeducation.com/mock_shop/latymer))
- **Examberry:** GL-style mock days (Bucks, Slough, Tiffin, Sutton, independents) with video explanations and ranking stats; results in 5 working days; prices via booking flow/phone ([mocks.examberry.com](https://mocks.examberry.com/))
- **Teachitright:** invigilated school-hall mocks in Gerrards Cross, Maidenhead, Reading, Slough, Windsor; report within 3 days; brochure pricing ([Teachitright mocks](https://teachitright.com/11-plus-mock-exams/))

**Benchmark takeaway:** the going rate for a credible in-person GL mock is **£55–80 per sitting**, with volume discounts pulling multi-mock packages toward £50/sitting. None of these operators has an Oxford venue.

## 5. Practice paper packs (GL / CGP / Bond / EPP)

- **GL Assessment official:** free downloadable familiarisation papers; paid printed practice packs — 3 packs per subject (VR, NVR, English, maths), each containing 3 test papers + answer sheets + parent guidance, plus a 12-pack full bundle; sold direct and via Amazon ([GL Assessment 11+ shop](https://11plus.gl-assessment.co.uk/), [free materials](https://11plus.gl-assessment.co.uk/pages/free-materials), [12-pack bundle on Amazon](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Revised-2019-Assessment-Practice-Papers/dp/0708727719))
- **Bond (OUP):** assessment paper books **£7.35–£7.97** each (e.g. Maths 10-11+ Book 1 £7.35, VR 10-11+ Book 1 £7.97); 4-subject bundle **£29.55** (RRP £32) ([Amazon UK — Bond Maths](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Bond-11-Maths-Assessment-Papers/dp/019277641X), [Bond VR](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Bond-11-Verbal-Reasoning-Assessment/dp/0192740350), [Bond bundle](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Assessment-Papers-10-11-Years-Bundle/dp/0192778579))
- **CGP:** GL practice-paper packs of 4 tests per subject; 8-pack bundle (32 tests) and 12-pack "complete" bundle (48 tests); pack-level prices on cgpbooks.co.uk (typical CGP paper-pack price point ~£8–11/pack — not confirmed in listings) ([CGP 8-pack bundle](https://www.cgpbooks.co.uk/11-plus-13-plus-books/11/mixed/el6pbe3-11-gl-practice-paper-8-pack-bundle), [CGP 12-pack bundle](https://www.cgpbooks.co.uk/11-plus-13-plus-books/11/mixed/emv6pbe2-11-gl-complete-practice-paper-12))
- **Exam Papers Plus (premium PDF packs):** school-specific and subject packs with tutorial videos; comparable 12+ bundle listed at **£195 (down from £260)** — indicative of their premium three-figure bundle pricing ([EPP 12+ bundle](https://exampapersplus.co.uk/browse/papers/twelve-plus/12-plus-bundle/), [EPP 11+ range](https://exampapersplus.co.uk/browse/papers/eleven-plus/))
- **The Exam Coach:** Year 6 revision/paper products around **£24.99** on sale ([Exam Coach store](https://www.theexamcoach.tv/courses)); free 11+ paper libraries are common lead magnets (Exam Coach, CGP, Examberry Papers) ([CGP free tests](https://www.cgpbooks.co.uk/info/preparing-for-the-11-plus-with-cgp/free-11-plus-practice-tests), [Examberry Papers free GL](https://examberrypapers.co.uk/resources/free-11-plus-practice-papers/gl-assessment/))

**Benchmark takeaway:** print papers are a **£7–12 per book** commodity dominated by Bond/CGP/GL; the premium is captured digitally — EPP proves parents pay **£150–260 for a bundle** when it's school-specific with video walkthroughs.

## Cross-cutting pricing patterns

**Regional premium.** The South East commands roughly a 30–60% uplift on national 1:1 rates (£60–80 vs £40–55 national average; £80–120 for super-selective specialists) ([TutorLab](https://www.tutorlab.uk/research/private-tutor-rates-uk)). Oxford's *marketplace* rates (£27–32/hr) sit oddly below this — closer to national average — while its agency rates (£70+) match London. The premium tier exists but is served remotely, not locally.

**Subscription vs one-off.** Platforms discount heavily for commitment: Atom annual −20% ([Atom pricing](https://www.atomlearning.com/pricing)), CGP yearly −30% ([CGP 11+ Online](https://www.cgpbooks.co.uk/11-plus-13-plus-books/11/11-plus-online-subscriptions)), BOFA fixed terms effectively halve the monthly rate ([BOFA offers](https://www.bofa11plus.com/content/offer-subscription)). Mock providers instead use **volume ladders** (Redmond £80 → £50/sitting; Learning Street 4-pack −20% ([Learning Street mocks](https://www.learningstreet.co.uk/11-plus-mock-exams/)); BOFA mocks only in packs of 3). The market trains parents to expect a meaningful multi-buy discount on mocks.

**Sibling/family discounts** are surprisingly rare and un-advertised among mock and platform providers (none surfaced at Redmond, Examberry, Atom, BOFA in this research); individual tutors advertise them informally ([Superprof Oxford](https://www.superprof.co.uk/lessons/11-school-exam-preparation/oxford/)). An explicit sibling policy is a cheap differentiator.

**Free-first funnels are universal.** GL's own free familiarisation papers, CGP/Exam Coach/Examberry free paper libraries, Explore Learning free trials, Teachitright free open sessions ([Teachitright open sessions](https://teachitright.com/11plus-open-sessions/)). A pre-launch brand should assume a free diagnostic mock or paper pack is table stakes for list-building.

**Useful anchor set for Summit pricing:** £9.50/mo floor (BOFA) · £69.99/mo ceiling (Atom top tier) · £35 online mock (Chuckra) · £80 in-person mock (Redmond) · £20–36 group session · £50–75/hr credible Oxford premium 1:1 · £299 holiday intensive (Exam Coach) · £2,750–4,500/yr total family spend on a tutored 11+ campaign.
