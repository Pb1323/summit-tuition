# SEO Keyword Opportunities — Summit Tuition (UK 11+ / GL-style mocks, Oxford)

*Research compiled July 2026 from live search results. Competition levels are judgements based on who actually ranks, not tool volumes. British context throughout.*

---

## 1. The Oxford strategic fact (verified)

**Oxfordshire has no state grammar schools.** The county went fully comprehensive in the 1970s — confirmed by the Eleven Plus Exams forum thread ["No Grammar School in Oxford"](https://www.elevenplusexams.co.uk/forum/11plus/viewtopic.php?t=43754) and by [The Exam Coach's Oxfordshire page](https://www.theexamcoach.tv/grammar-and-independent-schools/oxfordshire-all-schools). (Beware junk pages like [Eduscope's "Best Grammar Schools in Oxfordshire"](https://eduscope.uk/oxfordshire/grammar-schools) claiming "15 grammar schools" — these count independents and are a symptom of thin programmatic SEO in this niche, i.e. beatable competition.)

**Implications for keywords:**

- Local "11+" demand in Oxford is really two audiences:
  1. **Independent school entry** — Magdalen College School (MCS), Oxford High School, Headington, Abingdon, St Edward's, d'Overbroeck's etc. Each sets its **own** exam (MCS: VR + Maths + English + interview, January of Y6 — [Owl Tutors MCS guide](https://owltutors.co.uk/schools/magdalen-college-school/); Oxford High: computer-based assessment day in November + interview — [Pre-test.co.uk](https://www.pre-test.co.uk/content/oxford-high-school-admissions-familiarisation-mock-test); Headington: own test, late-November entrance day — [Exam Papers Plus](https://exampapersplus.co.uk/headington-school-11-plus-11-exam-information/); Abingdon: part computer-based, Jan of Y6, incl. "creative comprehension" — [Exam Papers Plus](https://exampapersplus.co.uk/advice/11-plus-year-6/abingdon-school-11-plus-exam-information/)).
  2. **Out-of-county grammar applications** — chiefly the **Buckinghamshire Secondary Transfer Test** (GL Assessment, two multiple-choice papers, out-of-county registration May–June, test in September, qualifying score 121 — [Buckinghamshire Council](https://www.buckinghamshire.gov.uk/schools-and-learning/schools-index/school-admissions/grammar-schools-and-transfer-testing-11/)), plus Reading/Berkshire grammars (Reading School, Kendrick).
- So "11 plus tutor Oxford" searchers mostly need **independent-school prep or Bucks/Berks GL prep** — a nuance almost no ranking page addresses. Generic pages talk about "grammar school preparation in Oxford", which is literally wrong. This is Summit's authority wedge.
- Summit's GL-style mocks map perfectly to the Bucks/Berks out-of-county audience; school-specific mock/prep pages map to the independent audience.

## 2. National keyword clusters

### 2.1 "11 plus practice papers" / "free 11 plus papers" — competition: HIGH

**Who ranks:** [Exam Papers Plus](https://exampapersplus.co.uk/), [Examberry Papers](https://examberrypapers.co.uk/resources/free-11-plus-practice-papers/gl-assessment/), [GL Assessment's own shop](https://11plus.gl-assessment.co.uk/pages/free-materials), [SATs Papers](https://www.sats-papers.co.uk/11-plus-papers/), [KSOL](https://www.ksol.co.uk/contents/11plus-free-downloads/11plus-papers), [Collins](https://collins.co.uk/pages/revision-collins-11-free-samples), [11PluseHelp](https://www.11plusehelp.co.uk/), [Pass 11 Plus Grammar](https://pass11plusgrammar.co.uk/11-plus-free-practice-papers), Bond, CGP.

**Reasoning:** established publishers and dedicated paper shops with years of links and thousands of indexed pages. Head terms are not winnable pre-launch. *Use* free papers as a lead magnet, but target long-tails ("free GL style verbal reasoning paper with answers PDF") rather than the head.

### 2.2 "GL assessment mock exam" / "11 plus mock exam online" — competition: MEDIUM

**Who ranks:** [mocks.elevenplusexams.co.uk](https://mocks.elevenplusexams.co.uk/) (the forum's own mock arm — dominant), [Chuckra 11plus.co.uk](https://www.11plus.co.uk/), [11plussuccess.co.uk](https://11plussuccess.co.uk/11-plus-mock-exam/), [11 Plus VIP](https://www.11plusvip.com/11plus-mock-exams), [11 Plus London](https://11pluslondon.co.uk/mock-exams/) (£60/subject), plus small regional operators ([elevenplusmock.org.uk](https://elevenplusmock.org.uk/), [Mock Exam Centre](https://mockexamcentre.co.uk/), [Sehyog](https://www.sehyog.co.uk/11plus-mock-test)).

**Reasoning:** the head is contested by elevenplusexams.co.uk, but the long tail is fragmented among small, dated sites with weak UX. "GL mock exam online with instant feedback", "11 plus mock exam what score is good", "online vs in-person 11 plus mock" are all winnable. Note most incumbents sell *in-person* mocks; polished online GL-style mocks with diagnostics is an underserved angle.

### 2.3 "GL vs CEM" explainers — competition: MEDIUM, but strategically shifting

**Who ranks:** [Collins](https://collins.co.uk/blogs/11-advice-for-parents/what-are-the-main-differences-between-the-two-11-exam-boards-gl-and-cem), [Bond](https://www.bond11plus.co.uk/11-plus-cem-gl), [Twinkl](https://www.twinkl.com/blog/what-are-the-key-differences-between-the-gl-and-the-cem-11-exam-boards-that-tutors-need-to-be-aware-of), [Atom Learning](https://resources.atomlearning.co.uk/en/knowledge/what-is-the-difference-between-gl-cem-and-other-types-of-11-plus-exams), [SATs Arcade](https://www.satsarcade.co.uk/guides/gl-vs-cem-11-plus), assorted tutor blogs.

**Reasoning:** lots of content but most is stale (CEM has exited paper 11+ testing; regions have migrated to GL). A *current* "which exam board does my region use in 2026/27" page — GL vs CEM vs ISEB vs school-own papers — outdates most of the ranking content. Medium effort, high authority payoff, feeds mock-exam conversion.

### 2.4 "When to start 11 plus preparation" / timeline content — competition: LOW–MEDIUM

**Who ranks:** [11plusguide.com](https://www.11plusguide.com/11-plus-exam-preparation/general-advice/start/11-plus-year-4-preparation/), [Bond](https://www.bond11plus.co.uk/11-plus-when-to-start), [Explore Learning](https://www.explorelearning.co.uk/free-resources/when-to-start-preparing-for-11-plus-exams/), [Atom](https://www.atomlearning.com/blog/11-plus-guide), [The Exam Coach](https://www.theexamcoach.tv/the-blog/11-plus-exam-help-a-detailed-18-point-guide) — plus Mumsnet threads ranking directly.

**Reasoning:** ranking content is generic listicle advice; Mumsnet threads outrank many commercial pages, which signals Google wants authentic, specific answers. A year-by-year series ("Year 4 11 plus plan", "starting 11 plus prep in Year 5 — are you too late?", "6 months to the 11+ — intensive plan") written in parents' own language (see file 03 language bank) is a clear gap. Long-tails like "have I left it too late 11 plus" currently return only forum threads — no commercial page answers it.

### 2.5 "11 plus tutoring cost" — competition: LOW–MEDIUM

**Who ranks:** [Atom Learning's cost breakdown](https://www.atomlearning.com/blog/11-plus-tutoring-cost) plus Mumsnet threads ([example](https://www.mumsnet.com/talk/am_i_being_unreasonable/5201637-to-ask-how-much-are-you-paying-for-11-plus-tuition)).

**Reasoning:** only one strong commercial page (Atom's, which is self-serving toward its subscription). An honest regional price guide ("what 11+ tutoring costs in Oxford in 2026: 1:1 vs group vs online vs mocks") is winnable and converts by cost-anchoring Summit's price.

## 3. Oxford-local keyword clusters

### 3.1 "11 plus tutor Oxford" / "11 plus tuition Oxford" — competition: MEDIUM (directories, no strong local specialist)

**Who ranks:** [Superprof](https://www.superprof.co.uk/lessons/11-school-exam-preparation/oxford/) (from £15/hr, avg ~£27), [11plustutoring.uk](https://www.11plustutoring.uk/11-plus-exams/town/11-plus-tutoring-oxford/) (programmatic town page), [Oxford Tutors](https://oxfordtutors.com/) (broad agency, not 11+-focused), [Tutor Hunt](https://www.tutorhunt.com/subjects/eleven-plus/locations/oxford/), [Tutorful](https://tutorful.co.uk/search/eleven-plus-11/tutors-in/oxford) (avg £32/hr), [The Exam Coach Oxford page](https://www.theexamcoach.tv/11-plus-tuition/oxford), [Teachers To Your Home](https://www.teacherstoyourhome.com/uk/oxford/11-plus-tutor), [Adnan Khan Tutoring](https://www.adnankhantutoring.co.uk/11-plus-tuition-oxford/), even the [Oxfordshire Family Information Service directory](https://fisd.oxfordshire.gov.uk/kb5/oxfordshire/directory/service.page?id=HeoxKRBZxZ4).

**Reasoning:** page one is marketplaces and programmatic location pages — *no dominant Oxford-native 11+ specialist*. Several ranking pages wrongly frame Oxford 11+ as grammar-school prep. A genuinely local page that says "Oxfordshire has no grammar schools — here's what the 11+ actually means in Oxford (MCS, Oxford High, Headington, Abingdon, or Bucks/Berks grammars)" beats them on relevance and E-E-A-T. **Highest-priority local target.**

### 3.2 School-specific prep pages ("MCS 11+", "Headington 11+", "Abingdon School 11+", "Oxford High 11+") — competition: LOW–MEDIUM, high intent

**Who ranks:** national tutor agencies with programmatic school pages — [Owl Tutors](https://owltutors.co.uk/schools/magdalen-college-school/), [Exam Papers Plus](https://exampapersplus.co.uk/headington-school-11-plus-11-exam-information/), [examtutor.co.uk](https://www.examtutor.co.uk/school/magdalen-college-school-mcs-11/), [BlackStone Tutors](https://www.blackstonetutors.com/magdalen-college-school-11-past-papers-and-answers/), [PiAcademy](https://piacademy.co.uk/blog/magdalen-college-school/), [Taylor Tuition](https://www.taylor-tuition.co.uk/blog/magdalen-college-school-11-admissions), [Atom](https://www.atomlearning.com/blog/magdalen-college-school), [Pre-test.co.uk](https://www.pre-test.co.uk/content/oxford-high-school-admissions-familiarisation-mock-test), plus the schools' own admissions pages.

**Reasoning:** everyone ranking is a *London-based* generalist running templated school pages; none is in Oxford and none offers local mock days. Summit pages combining exact current exam format + dates + a school-style mock ("MCS 11+ mock exam, Oxford") would be the only locally credible option. Low volume per page but very high intent and a defensible moat. Build for: MCS, Oxford High, Headington, Abingdon, St Edward's, plus "Oxford independent school 11+ entrance exams compared".

### 3.3 "11 plus mock exam Oxford" / "11 plus test centre Oxfordshire" — competition: LOW (near-vacant)

**Who ranks:** almost nobody local. Nearest physical mock centres surfaced are **Beaconsfield (Bucks), Reading, or Edgware** ([Chuckra venue page](https://www.11plus.co.uk/venues/buckinghamshire-beaconsfield-test-centre/)); otherwise generic national mock providers ([OxBridge Centre](https://oxbridgeuk.com/11-plus-mock-tests/) — despite the name, not Oxford-specific in results; [Pass Eleven Plus](https://passelevenplus.co.uk/11-plus-mock-exams/)).

**Reasoning:** Oxford families currently drive to Reading or Beaconsfield for in-person mocks, or use online mocks. "11 plus mock exam Oxford" is effectively an open SERP — the clearest quick win in the entire keyword set, and it is Summit's core product.

### 3.4 Bucks/Berks out-of-county cluster ("Buckinghamshire 11 plus from Oxfordshire", "out of county grammar school application") — competition: LOW

**Who ranks:** [Buckinghamshire Council's own pages](https://www.buckinghamshire.gov.uk/schools-and-learning/schools-index/school-admissions/grammar-schools-and-transfer-testing-11/), [Atom's Bucks guide](https://www.atomlearning.com/blog/buckinghamshire-11-plus), [BlackStone](https://www.blackstonetutors.com/buckinghamshire-grammar-schools-secondary-transfer-test-11-exam-information/), [Headstart11plus](https://www.headstart11plus.co.uk/post/buckinghamshire-secondary-transfer-test-11-review-and-appeal-process).

**Reasoning:** no one targets the *Oxfordshire parent applying out-of-county*. A guide — "Applying to Bucks/Berks grammar schools from Oxfordshire: registration dates (May–June), the GL Secondary Transfer Test in September, the 121 qualifying score, catchment realities" — is unowned and pairs exactly with GL-style mocks. Same play for Reading School/Kendrick.
